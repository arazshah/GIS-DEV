**How to Apply**

To ensure each student is well-prepared and committed to learning, our enrollment process includes a simple initial assessment. Here’s how it works:

1. **Step 1**: Send an email expressing your interest in the course.
2. **Step 2**: You’ll receive a set of three to five questions related to your knowledge, learning goals, and experience.
3. **Step 3**: Based on your responses, you’ll be invited to join the course.

This approach helps us tailor the learning experience to students who are truly committed and ready to engage.
